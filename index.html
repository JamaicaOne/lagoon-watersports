Luminous Lagoon

Located just outside Montego Bay in Falmouth, a phenomenon occurs where microorganisms called dinoflagellates emit blue luminous light when activated. Come experience the bioluminescence that exists only in certain parts of the world. This is a fun night boat tour, where you are welcome to jump in and splash around to see it up close, or you can watch from the comfort of the boat. Local bar and restroom/changing room available.
